I am a Senior Student of [Chemistry-Bachelor's Degree](https://fq.uh.cu/docencia/pregrado/) at the [University of Havana](http://www.uh.cu/) since 2017. In Spring 2019, I started to work in the Laboratory of Computational and Theoretical Chemistry, lead by [Prof. Dr. Luis Alberto Montero](https://scholar.google.es/citations?user=ZZIpOQwAAAAJ&hl=es). There I use theory and computational methods to explore the transition states of organic reactions to understand the mechanistic features of the reaction. My work is under supervision of [Gerardo Manuel Ojeda Carralero, M.Sc.](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=6RrCA68AAAAJ&hl).
